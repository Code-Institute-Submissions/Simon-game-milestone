Instructions | Simon Game®

  1. Click the Start Button. This will start the sequence of the game, please pay attention to the sound, this will help you better identify the correct tile you need to click.
  2. Click the buttons that were highlighted, in the order in which they were highlighted.
  3. You will earn one point for each sequence that you get correct, try to reach your max score
  4. You can stop the game at any time by clicking stop, this will also reset the counter if you wish to start over
